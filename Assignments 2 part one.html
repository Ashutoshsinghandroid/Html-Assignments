Html entities:

The HTML Entities are strings of text that start with an ampersand symbol(&) and end with a semicolon(;). These entities are commonly used to demonstrate characters that are reserved (otherwise, they would be interpreted as HTML code) and imperceptible characters such as non-breaking spaces.
for example:- Copyright ,Greater than ,Less than ,Double quotes ,Registration mark ,Trademark symbol & , © , > , ™ , ®

Web Accessibility:-

Web accessibility refers to the practice of designing and developing web applications that can be accessed and used by people with disabilities or different needs, without barriers or limitations.
some devices:- Screen Reader , Voice recognition software, eye traking devices

Ways to improve Accessibility in Html:

Text Content

Having well-structured content that includes headings, paragraphs, and lists is one of the most helpful accessibility features for users who rely on screen readers

Page Layouts

Although it is possible to design a layout using nested,div elements, it is preferable to utilise proper sectioning elements to encapsulate your main navigation , footer, repeated content units articles, and other relevant content.
